ABOUT US

Pest Cemetery BD is a Full Stack Insects Solution Company starts to run since 2013 in Malaysia named ZR termitese. After successful business operation in Malaysia our Head of Operation Mr. Riaz Ahmed, popular Pest technician in Malaysia, decided to serve his excellence in Bangladesh. In 2018 we started our journey in our dreamland Bangladesh.

We came to know that so many companies are providing pest control service in Dhaka city but none of these company using proper guideline and safety system. We decided to study more about current scenario of this industry and successfully we have done this study. Our Head of Operation Mr. Riaz Ahmed went back to Malaysia & Singapore for training and his knowledge development organized by Clark Pest Remedy, an American Pest Control Company, and he came back in 2020 with huge knowledge & experience of practical field.

We decided to use American Technology along with American Chemical. We have very special care to client’s choice so that you can make your package independently. Talk to us ahead of planning insects control in your House, Office, Factory, warehouse and your business premises. We will assist you to have an insect’s free environment with our 360 degree service.

Our Mission

To provide an environmentally friendly and effective approach to pest control services that is reliable, effective and sustainable through monitoring Techniques as outlined in our Integrated Pest Management system (IPM) and achieve overall process excellence.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred pest control company providing integrated pest management solutions to both residential, commercial and industrial properties that safeguard our environment, human health and provide peace of mind to our customers.
